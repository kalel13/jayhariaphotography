About Me


A sentence which connects me to photography.
"If you want to learn what someone fears losing, watch what they photograph!"

Hi, welcome to my photography world. Let me introduce myself. I am Jay Haria, a Mumbai based photographer. My story is like most of the photographers, I'm an IT Engineer. Enough said?

A picture speaks a thousand words, right? That's my goal when it comes to photography. I wish to capture hundreds of thousands of words in my pictures, various emotions, feelings & stories which even words cannot express. This is my form of self expression.

I first started this back in 2014, when my dad brought a basic Nikon camera. After playing with various settings, buttons and so many equipments, now I'm a professional photographer in the fields of street, nature, architecture, portraits, pets, etc. I also shoot for weddings, parties and college events. Basically I get paid for shooting people ( not literally, this is not America ).
Take your time to browse through my plethora of portfolio. I hope you find something that connects to you.
